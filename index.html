Founded in the year 2009, ROBODEX is the Robotics club of Delhi Public School Dwarka, New Delhi. The club began with only 5 members as intra-house activity. We grew to conduct our first inter school event in the year 2010
Becoming a part of this exciting community will provide you unique opportunities to showcase your skills and talents! Our team offers a wide range of inter-school and college level competitions, giving you the chance to gain invaluable experience.
